ITHACA, NY

No matter the season, come and explore the beauty of Ithaca, NY, located at the southern tip of majestic Cayuga Lake. Ithaca is a fun-loving, worldly connected city with something for everyone, be it indoors or outdoors; explore the gorges, falls and parks, walk Cornell & Ithaca College campuses, visit the many museums, science centers, nature centers and don’t forget the county’s best farmers market.
Did you know Ithaca was know as the Hollywood of the East? Yep, between 1914 to 1919 the Wharton brothers shot feature, short and series silent films here.
